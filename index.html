What is MEPA?
A virtual Community of Practice and branch of Project ECHO connecting natural history museum biorepositories with biomedical initiatives across the Americas.
About the program

Bridging museums,
genomics, and global health

MEPA aims to improve the sustainability, rigor, and replicability of pathogen surveillance in wild animals. In collaboration with Universidad de San Carlos de Guatemala, we lead hands-on Field+Genomics Workshops where participants conduct field-based surveillance of small mammals, perform DNA extractions and library preparation, run Nanopore Adaptive Sampling, and carry out basic bioinformatics — all in the field.
